SimpleFlights began as inistially as set of Apple Shortcut actions in a lengthy workflow that I created to help me fill out my logbook and to share my license training progress with friends and family, but has since evolved into a standalone Flutter app once I realized that I wanted to have more capabilities to help suppliment my PTR, share flight updates, and help my flight instructors get more insight into my flights as a student pilot.

Whether you're in the cockpit or on the couch...

Here are some of the things you can do with SimpleFlights!

Record Key Flight Details

SimpleFlights makes it easy to log your flights, ICAO codes, Hobbs/Air/Flight times, and flight paths at 1Hz precision. After the flight, you can then playback the flight path overlayed on the map!

Manage Your Fleet

Keey track of owned or rented aircraft that you fly. SimpleFlights makes it easy for you to manage and update fleet info to supplement your PTR or logbook.

420KB/Hr Efficiency

Optimized GPS thining means years of flight logs take up less space than a single high-res photo, when your storage starts to matter, we make it easy to manage your on-device app data without compromising logs.

Stream Your Flight Live!

Share a live view of your current flight to a passenger in the cabin, or over a TLS secured stream to someone following along from home. Your flightpath is overlayed onto an openstreetview map with a robust set of highway, railway, waterway data, VOR's and Airports able to be selectively toggled to view.

Planning an XC?

SimpleFLights leverages and integrates public and opensource data to pull METAR, TAF, Frequencies, Runway orientations and lengths, navigation aides into one easy to find place. Whether you're enroute, or on the ramp you can plan accordingly.

"Text me when you land!"

Does your instructor, friend or family member want to know you've safely arrived at your destination? With one tap you can share your key flight info into a ready to send RCS with the option to include a map view of your flight path!


With plenty of features already coming soon to make your flights simple!
